Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom have been engaged for almost five years now, and fans have been wondering when the couple will finally tie the knot. Recently, Perry sparked rumors that she and Bloom had secretly gotten married after she was spotted with a new ring on an important finger.

Are Katy Perry, Orlando Bloom Married?

Bloom proposed to Perry on Valentine’s Day back in 2019, gifting her with an engagement ring made to look like a flower, featuring a ruby surrounded by diamond petals. Recently, however, Perry was seen sporting a different ring on that finger.

According to the Daily Mail, Perry and Bloom were recently spotted arriving home in Los Angeles after a New Year’s vacation. Perry kept her look casual in a gray sweatsuit, but she had two rings on her left hand. One was a diamond ring on her index finger, and the other was a gold band on her ring finger.

The new jewelry has sparked rumors that Perry and Bloom might have secretly tied the knot. The couple first started dating back in 2016, and they welcomed a daughter named Daisy Dove in 2020. Did they finally say “I do”?

It’s Not the First Marriage Rumor

This isn’t the first time Perry and Bloom have been rumored to have secretly tied the knot. Last year, Perry’s word choice during a speech honoring Bloom’s ex-wife Miranda Kerr had people wondering if the couple was married.

Perry presented an award to Kerr, calling her a “sister from another mister.” She added that they broke the “ex-wife, new wife narrative,” leading fans to speculate that Perry and Bloom were now husband and wife. However, it seems it was just a reference to the couple’s engagement.
