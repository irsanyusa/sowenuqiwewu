The cast for Dancing with the Stars Season 31 hasn’t been officially announced yet, but several celebrities have been rumored to be among the new contestants. That’s not even including TikTok star Charlie D’Amelio, whose involvement was reported earlier this week.

Which Celebs Are Rumored to Be on Dancing with the Stars?

Just Jared shared a list of several celebrities who have been rumored to appear on the next season of DWTS. That includes Good Morning America correspondent Dr. Jennifer Ashton. She and pro dancer Alan Bersten recently followed each other on Twitter.

Other rumored contestants include Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Sutton Stracke (according to a blind item), and Lab Rats actor Spencer Boldman (who tweeted a dancing emoji in response to a comment about his “next project”).

But perhaps the most notable name attached to the upcoming season is Robert Irwin, son of late wildlife expert Steve Irwin. Robert’s sister Bindi Irwin previously won Season 21 of the show with partner Derek Hough.

In January, Robert told Entertainment Tonight of joining the show at some point, “We’ve gotta do that, don’t we? Yeah, I reckon it’s about time, eh?” He added that he would “definitely give it a go” and told his sister, “I reckon you could give me some tips. I think it’d be a lot of fun.”

Charli D’Amelio Is Reportedly Joining the Show

Earlier this week, it was reported that TikTok star Charli D’Amelio and her mother Heidi would both be competing against one another on Season 31 of DWTS. Charli has over 145 million followers on the platform, and she trained as a dancer from a young age.

Charli also has a connection to judge Derek Hough. She appeared on the television special Step Into…The Movies With Derek and Julianne Hough earlier this year. Within the special she participated in a dance routine inspired by Dirty Dancing.

Dancing with the Stars Season 31 premieres September 19 on Disney+. The new cast will reportedly be announced September 8 on Good Morning America.
