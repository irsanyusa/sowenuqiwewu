Some super fans love to learn everything they can about their favorite talent show judges, including their relationship status! Here’s a list of talent show judges that aren’t married in 2022.

Although these celebrities haven’t tied (or re-tied) the knot, it doesn’t mean they aren’t dating at the moment. Here’s everything to know about their previous relationship and if they’re seeing anyone currently.

6. Derek Hough

This Dancing With the Stars judge has been very candid about his proposal. Hough popped the question to Hayley Erbert after nearly seven years of dating the fellow dancer. He had teased their proposal for years and admitted that they weren’t in a rush to walk down the aisle.

5. Demi Lovato

Former X Factor judge Demi Lovato is currently single in 2022. This singer previously got engaged to Young and The Restless star Max Ehrich in July 2020. Their relationship ended soon after in September 2020, a source close to Lovato said that they “didn’t think he had good intentions” and “there were so many red flags.”

4. Robin Thicke

The Masked Singer panelist Robin Thicke is currently engaged to model April Love Geary. This couple began dating in 2014, they currently have three children together, Mia Love Thicke (4), Luca Patrick Thicke (1), and Lola Alain Thicke (3). This will be Thicke’s second marriage.

3. Katy Perry

American Idol judge Katy Perry has been engaged to Orlando Bloom since 2019. They first got together in January of 2016, only to split in February 2017. Perry and Bloom got back together in March 2018 and announced their engagement the next year. They welcomed their first child together in August 2020. This will be Perry’s second marriage.

2. Camila Cabello

The Voice coach Camila Cabello is currently single in 2022. Cabello dated Shawn Mendes from July 2019 until November 2021. According to their joint statement, they want to remain friends in their next chapters of life. Reports state that Cabello and Mendes became friends in early 2014, but didn’t become close friends until the following year.

1. Simon Cowell

This America’s Got Talent judge has always said he doesn’t want to marry, but he recently changed his mind. Cowell popped the question to his longtime girlfriend, Lauren Silverman on Christmas Eve 2021. He proposed while on vacation with his family in Barbados and popped the question in front of his seven year old son Eric, as well as his step son Adam Silverman. The talent show mogul is keeping his wedding plans private.
