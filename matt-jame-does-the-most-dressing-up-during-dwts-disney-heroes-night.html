‘Honey! Where is my super suit?’ Matt James has it. The Bachelor star dressed up as Frozone (complete with Frozone’s iconic glasses) from The Incredibles for Disney Heroes night this week on Dancing with the Stars.

James and his partner Lindsay Arnold danced the Quickstep to “The Incredits” from The Incredibles (aka the movie’s theme song.) Arnold dressed as Edna Mode to stay on-theme in a hilariously adorable black bob wig.

James said he chose to dress as Frozone since people tell him “all the time” that he looks like the cartoon character. He wore the full superhero unitard and posed in various well-known Frozone poses as Arnold danced as Edna.

“It looks like you’re defrosting nicely,” said judge Bruno Tonioli after the routine. Tonioli has been thirsting after James all season, so it’s no surprise he loved James’s skintight supersuit.

James Dresses as Frozone for DWTS Disney Heroes Night

“Any man who can do that in a unitard and still make me smile like that, God bless you Matt James!” said judge Carrie Ann Inaba.

While the judges all agreed it was not a perfect dance, James and Arnold still entertained and stuck to the theme. They earned a final score of 26 out of 40 for the night placing them right in the middle of the performers so far tonight.

The two will perform the Paso Doble tomorrow night for Villain Night to “Jungle” by X Ambassadors and Jamie N Commons inspired by The Jungle Book.

Voting is now open and will stay open until the end of the episode.
