London, United Kingdom - All 380 matches to be played in the upcoming 2024/2025 English Premier League (EPL) season and the dates were released on Tuesday, June 18.

For the second season in a row, Arsenal will begin their quest for the league title with a game at Emirates Stadium. 'The Gunners' will play Wolves.

2024-25 season: Arsenal fans share expectation

Following the release of the fixtures, some Arsenal fans in Nigeria spoke to Legit.ng and shared their expectations.

Despite finishing second in the last two seasons, supporters of the North London club are hopeful of the EPL crown in the 2024-25 season.

Femi Badmus told Legit.ng:

"Based on the progress of the past few seasons and the clear path forged by Mikel Arteta and Edu, expectation for the next is at least a battle for the title again (the goal is to win by the way), a domestic cup challenge, and a far run in the Champions League."The new Champions League format might spring up some surprises, but the squad is being built to take up anything that is thrown at them."

For Toheeb Adetunji, he wants the team to keep up the form of last season.

Adetunji said:

"I am expecting some additions to the team, a non-injury striker. And they should keep the form from last season. Probably, we (will) win the league this season."
