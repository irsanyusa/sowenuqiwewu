Advantages of having a small penis

July 27, 2024
blog

Average penis sizes vary across races and regions, but factors like genetics and diet may also play a role in which side of the average you fall on.

While there is no ideal penis size or standardised metrics to classify whether you have a big or small penis, a significant amount of men believe their penis is smaller than normal.

Studies show that about 45% of men believe they have a small penis and wish they had it bigger. Many even seek treatments through penis enlargement products or surgeries to remedy their perceived small penis.

However, if you are one of those who wish they had a bigger penis, you may have a rethink after you learn of the advantages of having a smaller penis.

5 advantages of having a small penis

Here are some perks of having a smaller penis;

1. Easier concealment

Having a smaller penis makes it easier for you to conceal it in clothing. You don't need to worry about the discomfort that comes with bulges in certain clothes. You can even decide to free-ball (go out without underwear).

2. Reduced risk of certain injuries

If you have a smaller penis, you are less likely to suffer certain penis injuries or conditions like penile fracture, and discomfort during vigorous sexual activity.

3. More versatility during sex

Smaller penises may allow for greater flexibility in trying different sexual positions that may not be as comfortable or feasible with larger penises, leading to exploration and variety in the bedroom.

4. Increased comfort during sex

A smaller penis poses less discomfort to your partner during intercourse, making the process more enjoyable for you and your partner.

5. More focus on other forms of intimacy

With a smaller penis, you may invest more time and effort in developing other aspects of sexual intimacy, such as communication, foreplay, and creative techniques, which can lead to a more fulfilling sexual experience for you and your partner.

Remember, a smaller penis size is not a problem, and you should not feel insecure about it. You can have a fulfilled and healthy sexual relationship with a smaller penis through mutual respect and communication with your partner.
