Gwen Stefani could’ve been the winning coach of The Voice season 24, but her chance was lost when BIAS was voted off. The pop superstar recently reacted on the elimination, then hinting BIAS’ future endeavor.

Gwen Stefani is Sad About BIAS’ Elimination on The Voice

After season 24 Semifinals, Stefani took the time to reflect on what transpired during the episode. The singer posted her reaction via an Instagram Story post.

“I’m in backstage of The Voice. I don’t know if this is the first time that I’ve ever been to the Finals with nobody. Especially after, like, BIAS is just so cool and so real and genuine and authentic, all the things I’ve been saying. I am sad,” she said.

Yet, while Stefani admits that she’s heartbroken about the results of the Semifinals, she’s not bitter about it. As a matter of fact, she’s rather looking forward for BIAS’ next music venture.

“I just want to say, thank you for watching. Whoever did vote, thank you. Bias, I’ll see you in Tishomingo,” she said.

With this little teaser, it probably won’t be long until we see BIAS on stage again. Be that as it may, it’s valid for fans to be sure that they’ll soon witness the country singer perform on Blake Shelton’s Ole Red Tishomingo.

On Monday night, BIAS performed fan-selected “Bless the Broken Road” by Rascal Flatts. According to the singer, if it’s his time to walk out of The Voice, he wanted to make sure he represented himself well.

“As the last standing member of Team Gwen, you want her to have a chance in the finale along with the other coaches, and, for me, that also means that I get an opportunity to perform with her and do a song on TV. That’s something that I would love to do. But I think God just wanted to go out there and give it my best performance,” he said.

Stefani Stunned in a Sparkly Green Dress Amid Semifinals

During the Semifinals, Stefani looked gorgeous in her shimmering green ensemble. The singer’s sparkly outfit was made even better with its fitted long sleeves and plunging neckline. Stefani then matched the dress with a pair of knee-high boots and fishnet tights.

To elevate the look, she let her blonde locks down, perfectly matching the same with an eye-heavy makeup look. She further accessorized her outfit with chunky rings, a rhinestone choker necklace, and dangly earrings.

The pop star dropped the whole outfit on Instagram, triggering fans to express their compliments.

“Is this woman aging backwards?” one fan wrote.

“You always look amazing but I think tonight’s look is my favorite on you all season. Green is your color!” one user wrote.

“You should wear your hair like this more often!” another fan wrote.

Throughout The Voice season 24, Stefani has been very keen and detailed about her fashion looks. Surely, the coach is preparing for her Finale outfits, and we’re definitely excited to see how she’ll slay the red chair.
