Spotting clues within weeks, Dancing With The Stars fans seem convinced that Gabby Windey and Erich Schwer have broken up. The couple neither confirmed nor denied the speculations, thus leaving fans in the state of limbo.

Gabby Windey, Erich Schwer Allegedly Break Up

During Michael Bublé night of Dancing With The Stars, Gabby Windey and Val Chmerkovskiy fired up the ballroom with their sensual moves to “Home.” However, amid the performance, fans didn’t spare Gabby from a noticeable difference in her package.

The Bachelorette star wasn’t wearing her engagement ring throughout the live episode. This was unusual for her, since she’s consistently sported it since their TV engagement. Furthermore, fans noticed that Schwer was absent that night, fortifying fans’ allegations that the couple called it quits.

Some Reddit users also noticed that Gabby has not been wearing her ring not only on DWTS, but also outside the show. When she attended a Halloween party with a few DWTS cast, Gabby’s ring was nowhere to be spotted.

“It’s one thing to not [wear the ring] while dancing but not doing it while out seems to follow the trend of other break ups we’ve seen,” one user wrote.

A closer inspection of the issue would understandably lead fans to doubt the status of their relationship. Gabby normally filled her Instagram feed with photos of her and Schwer, but she hasn’t posted any sweet photo of them together for over a week. The same goes for her fiancé.

Some Fans Think They Could Still Be Together 

While several believe that the couple have split up, it’s possible they haven’t. Some fans pointed out that the DWTS star and Schwer are still following each other on social media. Additionally, he has posted an Instagram story supporting Gabby in the competition.

Subsequently, after the Michael Bublé night, Gabby told reporters that Schwer has been “supportive” of her ballroom dances. The Bachelorette star also said that her soon-to-be husband understands the goal of their performance.

Moreover, Schwer has been liking Gabby-related posts both in DWTS’ and Val’s accounts. This past week, he also followed her on TikTok.

“They could be totally fine and just not want to showcase their relationship as much as they were,” one fan wrote. “Maybe they talked about being more private.”

On the heat of speculations, many agree that only time will tell the real catch between the pair. At the moment, Gabby is proving her worth in Dancing With The Stars, securing a 46 out of 50 in the latest episode.
