23. 4. 2023 | Fields of Study

Czech universities have become a popular destination for international students seeking quality higher education. With a wide range of study programs offered in various fields, it can be challenging for students to decide which program to pursue. This article will provide an overview of the most popular fields of study for international students at Czech universities.

  • Medicine and Healthcare

    • One of the most popular fields of study for international students in the Czech Republic is medicine and healthcare. The country has a long tradition of medical education and research, and its universities offer excellent programs in this field. Students can choose from programs in general medicine, dentistry, pharmacy, and other healthcare-related fields.

  • Business and Economics

    • Another popular field of study for international students in the Czech Republic is business and economics. The country has a well-developed business environment and is home to many international companies. Czech universities offer programs in business administration, finance, marketing, and other related fields.

  • Engineering

    • Engineering is also a popular field of study for international students in the Czech Republic. The country has a strong industrial base, and its universities offer excellent programs in various engineering fields, such as mechanical engineering, civil engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science.

  • Humanities and Social Sciences

    • Many international students also choose to study humanities and social sciences at Czech universities. The country has a rich cultural heritage and a vibrant arts scene, making it an attractive destination for students interested in fields such as history, literature, philosophy, and cultural studies.

  • Information Technology

    • Information technology is a rapidly growing field of study in the Czech Republic. The country has a thriving tech industry, and its universities offer programs in various IT fields, such as software engineering, data science, and cybersecurity.

    Overall, these fields of study offer great opportunities for international students seeking quality higher education at Czech universities. However, students should carefully consider their interests and career goals before choosing a program. It is also important to research the specific requirements and admission procedures for each program, as they may vary by university.
