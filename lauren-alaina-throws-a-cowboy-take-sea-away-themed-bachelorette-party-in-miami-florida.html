Country music star Lauren Alaina recently celebrated her upcoming wedding with a bachelorette party weekend in Miami, Florida. The singer dubbed the glamorous festivities “Cowboy Take Sea Away” as she prepares to marry her fiancé, Cam Arnold.

Inside Lauren Alaina’s Bachelorette Party

Alaina jetted off to Miami with her best girlfriends for the bachelorette celebration planned by Bach To Basic, a luxury party planning company. The weekend included a backyard picnic coordinated by The Pretty Picnic, a nightclub outing, poolside relaxation, games, and more.

The bride-to-be gave fans a glimpse into the fun through social media, sharing photos and videos decked out in chic white ensembles and custom cowboy hats in keeping with the weekend’s theme. Her bride squad honored Alaina’s upcoming nuptials by pulling out all the stops for an unforgettable bachelorette experience.

Channeling traditional bachelorette style, Alaina donned all-white looks throughout the Miami weekend. This included a white fringe jacket, shorts, and cowboy boots for the backyard picnic. She also rocked a white cowboy hat with her name on it, pearl-adorned pieces reading “Bride,” and other glam accessories.

The coordinating ensembles provided a clean, chic aesthetic for photos and memories. Alaina’s stylish outfits also nod to her Southern roots with a modern cowboy flair. She looked every bit the glowing bride-to-be.

Large Wedding Planned for the Couple

According to reports, Alaina and her fiancé Arnold are planning a sizable wedding. Though she initially wanted something smaller, her groom-to-be dreamed of a big traditional wedding.

“He wanted a big wedding and I did not want a big wedding, so we compromised and we’re having a gigantic wedding,” Alaina.

Arnold has reportedly been part of numerous wedding parties, so a grand affair seems fitting. He and Alaina, who met in 2022, will surely have a wedding for the books when they officially tie the knot soon.

While the country music star has opened up about her wedding plans, she has yet to publicly share her official wedding date. However, the festivities indicate it is likely soon approaching.

Alaina previously revealed she has her gown already picked out. She also recently shared she was “almost married” after holding a wedding shower attended by both families. So the big day seems to be drawing near as she celebrates with loved ones.
