11 Hottest black celebrity couples in Hollywood right now

May 31, 2024
blog

1.) Gabrielle Union, Dwayne Wade

The actress and her NBA star husband have been dating since 2009 and tied the knot in Miami in front of their closest friends and family in August 2014. The couple have continued to give us very sweet PDA moments over the years and shares even more during the holiday seasons.

ADVERTISEMENT

2.) Ciara, Russell Wilson

The newly engaged couple are slowly becoming the world's most inspirational couple after taking a "no sex before marriage" oath to teach youths they can also practice abstinence too. The pair shares their affection for each other everywhere they go and do a great job as parents to Ciara's son, Future Zahiri. We can’t wait to them become husband and wife.

3.) Meagan Good, Devon Franklin

They are one of the most admired Black couples in America. They released their new book, "The Wait: A Powerful Practice for Finding the Love of Your Life and the Life You Love" in 2016, and has been traveling all over America to spread their message of celibacy, faith, strength and love.

4.) Kelly Rowland, Tim Witherspoon

ADVERTISEMENT

The couple tied the knot in quiet wedding in Florida back December 2014 and now share an adorable son together. Even though we get to see less of Kelly and Tim together in public, the former 'Destiny's Child' singer says she loves to keep her private life private.

5.) Stephen and Ayesha Curry

The NBA power couple reportedly met as teens while in a church youth group in a small town in North Carolina and have held onto their faith despite Steph's meteoric rise to international fame.

6.) Barack Obama, Michelle Obama

They've been sweethearts since their university days but grew up to become America's first couple after Obama won the elections in 2009. They share two daughters together and do not get shy to share one or two kisses in the public eye.

ADVERTISEMENT

7.) Will and Jada Pinkett Smith

The couple met on set when the actress auditioned to play Smith's girlfriend on his sitcom 'The fresh Prince of Bel Air'. Their relationship grew from there and they got married in 1997. They've been a couple for two decades and share two kids including Smith's son before they married.

8.) Boris Kodjoe, Nicole Ari Parker

Their love for each other leaves many greening with envy. Both actors met on set of the movie 'Soul Food' and tied in 2005. They share three children while enjoying blissful careers.

9.) Beyonce and Jay Z

ADVERTISEMENT

When the hip hop mogul met the former Destiny's Child singer, it was a match made in hip hop heaven. Both  Houston-born Bey and Brooklyn-born Jay have survived trials and tribulations in their marriage but remain stronger, share a lovely daughter and have changed the game for pop and rap music.

10.) Alicia Keys and Swizz Beatz

These beautiful souls tied the knot back in 2010 and share two children together.. Including their young son who is fast becoming a music producer like his daddy. The music couple are known for their luxury music, vacations and incredible humanitarian duties.

11.) Lala and Carmelo Anthony

ADVERTISEMENT

The actress and her NBA star husband became a black household name after the TV personality's hit show, 'Lala's Full Court Life' hit the air. The couple share a son and are pursuing amazing careers each.
