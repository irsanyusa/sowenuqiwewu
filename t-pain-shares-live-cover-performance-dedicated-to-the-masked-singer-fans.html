The Masked Singer Season 1 winner T-Pain (aka Monster) recently shared a video with fans showing him performing various cover songs live. He dedicated the performance to Masked Singer viewers who loved hearing his real voice on the show.

The Masked Singer Winner T-Pain Shares Live Cover Performance

When T-Pain competed as Monster in the first season of The Masked Singer, fans were stunned to hear his natural singing voice without the addition of Auto-Tune. Earlier this year, T-Pain released an album called On Top of the Covers, showing off his real voice.

The singer recently delivered a live performance of the cover songs on that album, as well as a few extras. T-Pain also released an album version of the concert for fans to stream. He talked about his decision to share the covers on Instagram.

“For all the people who wanted an album of me singing live, this is my holiday gift to you,” T-Pain wrote to accompany a clip of his performance of “Stay with Me” by Sam Smith. “For the Tiny Desk & Masked Singer fans & everyone in between, you ain’t heard nothing yet.”

What Songs Did He Perform?

The original version of T-Pain’s album On Top of the Covers featured eight tracks — “A Change Is Gonna Come,” “Don’t Stop Believin’,” “Sharing the Night Together,” “Stay with Me,” “Tennessee Whiskey,” “That’s Life,” “War Pigs,” and an instrumental track called “Skrangs (in K Major sus).”

The live version of the album features a number of extra songs, including T-Pain’s own track “Buy U a Drank.” Other new songs include “Summertime,” “Shout,” “Never Too Much,” “American Woman,” “I Don’t Want to Be,” “This Is How We Do It,” “Klink,” “Crazy,” and “Black Ice.”

These live performances will undoubtedly be a treat for The Masked Singer fans who were impressed by T-Pain’s vocal talent when he competed on the show in 2019. You can watch the concert on YouTube and stream the tracks on platforms such as Spotify and Apple Music.
