Doja Cat (Rapper and Singer-Songwriter)

September 16, 2024
blog

Full Name: Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini
Profession: Rapper and Singer-Songwriter

Biography: Doja Cat is an American rapper and singer whose career started with releasing self-made music on Soundcloud as a teenager, she was then picked up by Kemosabe and RCA records and signed a record deal at sixteen.

Her career was only moderately successful, however, prior to the viral success of her novelty single 'Mooo!' in 2018. This song became an incredibly popular internet meme, and the video received over 578 million views.

She has since continued her career by capitalizing on the success of this single, releasing her second studio album Hot Pink in 2019. This album reached the top 10 and was followed by her third album, Planet Her (2021).

Born: October 21, 1995
Birthplace: Los Angeles, California, USA
Age: 28 years old

Generation: Millennial
Chinese Zodiac: Pig
Star Sign: Libra
