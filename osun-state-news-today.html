Osun State, Nigeria

Osun State is located in the southwestern region of Nigeria and is bounded by Kwara State to the northeast, Ekiti and Ondo States to the east, Ogun State to the south, and Oyo State to the west and northwest. The state was created on August 27, 1991, from the eastern part of Oyo State.

Population

As of 2016, Osun State had an estimated population of about 3.4 million people, making it the 19th most populous state in Nigeria.

Governors

Osun State has had several governors since its creation in 1991. Here is a list of administrators and governors of Osun State:

  • Leo Segun Ajiborisha, Administrator, August 1991 - January 1992
  • Isiaka Adetunji Adeleke, Governor, January 1992 - November 1993
  • Anthony Udofia, Administrator, 9 December 1993 - 22 August 1996
  • Anthony Obi, Administrator, 22 August 1996 - August 1998
  • Theophilus Bamigboye, Administrator, August 1998 - May 1999
  • Adebisi Akande, Governor, 29 May 1999 - 29 May 2003
  • Olagunsoye Oyinlola, Governor, 29 May 2003 - 27 November 2010
  • Rauf Aregbesola, Governor, 27 November 2010 - 27 November 2018
  • Adegboyega Oyetola, Governor, 27 November 2018 - 2022
  • Ademola Adeleke, current governor

    • Postal Code
