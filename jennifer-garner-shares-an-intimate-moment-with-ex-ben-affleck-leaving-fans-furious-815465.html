Jennifer Garner Shares an Intimate Moment With Ex Ben Affleck Leaving Fans Furious

Former power couple Ben Affleck and Jennifer Garner are known for being friendly co-parents. Their being spotted together with their kids isn’t new, but this time around, fans are wondering if the duo crossed the line. They were pictured sharing a hug and a sweet moment when Jennifer leaned into Ben when they were in his car.

A surprising reunion

Jennifer Garner and Ben Affleck, who separated in 2018, were spotted together in a car with their daughter Seraphina, who is now 14 years old. The photos captured the moment when they exchanged a warm embrace in the vehicle, sharing smiles and seemingly reconnecting on a personal level.

In the pics, Jennifer can be seen leaning forward to Ben, who was sitting in the driver’s seat.

The photos sparked mixed reactions from fans.

After images of the reunion surfaced online, fans took to social media platforms to express their opinions. Some criticized Jennifer Garner, accusing her of being a “homewrecker” and Ben Affleck of being unfaithful to his new wife, Jennifer Lopez. Others pointed out that it’s no big deal, “He had a right to see his ex-wife, they share a child. And the pic doesn’t show anything except Jennifer is in the back seat, not the front seat.”

Garner and Affleck often meet as they co-parent their three kids.

Jennifer Garner and Ben Affleck have been known for their amicable co-parenting relationship since their separation. They often are seen together when the kids are involved. And J.Lo is cool with it, just a few weeks before, Affleck, his daughter Violet, and Jennifer Lopez were spotted at an event together.

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck were spotted being lovey-dovey later.

Shortly after Ben and Garner’s photo became a topic of debate on social media, J. Lo was spotted hanging out with him at a flea market. She let her feelings for her husband be on full display as the couple was photographed with Jennifer being all over Ben Affleck.
