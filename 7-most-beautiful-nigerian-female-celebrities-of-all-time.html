7 most beautiful Nigerian female celebrities of all time

July 09, 2024

It's about having carriage, poise, and confidence in their own skin. It's about being comfortable with who they are, embracing their flaws, and projecting an image of self-assurance that resonates with their fans. A beautiful celebrity is one who exudes grace, charm, and elegance.

It’s important to remember that although we love to see our favourite stars shine and flaunt their assets, beauty is not everything. Talent, hard work, and determination are equally important, if not more so.

In the end, the most beautiful female celebrities are the ones who combine their physical attractiveness with their talent, personality, and inner beauty, creating a lasting impression on their fans that goes beyond skin-deep.

Choosing the most beautiful Nigerian female celebrities is no easy feat, given the abundance of stunning stars in the entertainment industry.

While we acknowledge that beauty is subjective and can vary greatly depending on personal preference, we have gone ahead to compile a list of seven Nigerian female celebrities who have caught our eye with their looks and talent. Here they are, in particular, in order:

1) Genevieve Nnaji

Genevieve is a timeless beauty. At 43 years old, she looks like she hasn't aged a day since she first graced our screens in the '90s. Her ageless beauty is the envy of many, and she is without a doubt, one of the most beautiful Nigerian celebrities of all time.

Fans were left in awe when she took a picture with her daughter, and it was hard to tell who was the mother and who was the daughter. It's clear that beauty runs in the family, and this only proves that Genevieve may have found the fountain of youth.

What's more impressive about Genevieve is that she looks beautiful with or without makeup. Her natural beauty shines through.

Genevieve is also an iconic figure in the Nollywood industry. She has won numerous awards and accolades for her acting prowess and has played a significant role in shaping the Nigerian movie industry into what it is today.

2) Adesua Etomi-Wellington

Adesua is a true embodiment of beauty, grace, and talent. Her beauty is the kind that makes you want to double-tap every photo she posts on social media.

It is undeniable, and it's only matched by her impressive talent. If you've seen her in movies like "The Wedding Party," "Falling," and "King of Boys," then you know exactly what we're talking about.

She is also a fitness enthusiast who spends hours in the gym every week, and it shows. Her toned, slim figure is the envy of many women. And even though she doesn't have the biggest curves or backside in the game, she still manages to turn heads wherever she goes.

Adesua recently became a mother, and you would never guess it by looking at her. Her figure is still as impressive as ever, and she's showing no signs of slowing down.

In fact, in her latest movie, "Gangs of Lagos," she delivered a performance that left audiences speechless. She's the kind of actress who can disappear into a role and make you forget that you're watching a movie. She’s that good.

Susu, as she’s so fondly called, is a true gem in the entertainment industry. Her beauty, talent, and dedication to her craft are all aspects that make her a force to be reckoned with. She's someone who deserves all the accolades she's been getting and more.

3) Beverly Naya

Beverly Naya is a gorgeous Nigerian actress and model who was born in London, UK, but later moved to Nigeria and kicked off her acting career in 2010. She has starred in popular movies and TV shows, including "Skinny Girl in Transit," "Before 30," and "The Wedding Party."

In 2019, she produced a documentary on Netflix titled "Skin," which highlights the experiences of black women in Nigeria who face discrimination because of their dark skin. In the documentary, they talk about the pressures of conforming to beauty standards that prioritise lighter skin.

ADVERTISEMENT

Beverly was inspired to make the film because she, too, had struggled with low self-esteem due to being bullied for her skin colour while growing up in the UK.

When asked about the motivation behind "Skin," she said, "I want to inspire young people to love themselves. And I decided to use my documentary, 'Skin,' because I didn't want to restrict my message to a small community. I knew it was important to get the message further."

We couldn't agree more. There's nothing more beautiful than someone who not only embraces their own beauty but also inspires others to do the same.

Beverly is a true inspiration, and we're grateful for her advocacy for self-love and acceptance. She's not only beautiful on the outside but also on the inside, and that in itself, is what true beauty is all about.

4) Tiwa Savage

Tiwa is one of the most beautiful and iconic women in Nigeria, and her influence on the industry cannot be ignored.

Not only is she an accomplished singer and songwriter, but she has also become a trailblazer in terms of challenging traditional expectations of how women should present themselves in Nigerian society. Tiwa isn't afraid to express herself through her fashion choices or the people she associates with.

Despite her boldness, Tiwa has also faced her fair share of struggles.

In recent years, there was a video that circulated online purportedly showing her in a state of undress, which was a major scandal, but Tiwa refused to let it hold her back, and she continued to pursue her passion for music, surprising many people who thought it might be the end of her career. She's a fighter, and we can't help but love and respect her for that.

One of the most impressive things about this star is her agelessness. Although she is over 40, she looks so young that many people refuse to believe it, and her beauty is often a topic of discussion among fans and critics alike.

Her style is versatile, and she has a reputation for being sexy, which only adds to her appeal. She has been in the entertainment industry for decades, and yet she continues to put out banging music every time.

Tiwa's been through it all, but she's never let it hold her back. She's a true inspiration, her strength and resilience are what make her beautiful both inside and out.

5) Omotola Jalade Ekehinde

ADVERTISEMENT

Omotola Jalade Ekehinde, also known as Omosexy, is a Nollywood icon whose ageless beauty makes her stand out from the crowd. Even after all these years, Omotola continues to defy age and looks as radiant as ever.

Omotola’s beauty, grace and poise reflect both on and off the screen. As an actress, she is known for her versatility and has starred in several blockbuster movies.

Her talent has earned her countless accolades, including the prestigious Africa Movie Academy Award. She has made a huge impact in Nollywood and the Nigerian entertainment industry cannot count its successes without her name being included.

Beyond her acting talent, she is also a philanthropist and a social change advocate. She was appointed as a United Nations WFP Ambassador in 2005 and has been involved in several campaigns to fight poverty and empower women.

Omotola is a true inspiration to women, and her inner beauty shines through in her outlook on life.

6) Nancy Isime

Nancy Isime is a Nigerian actress, model, and media personality who has gained a reputation for her beauty, talent, and resilience in the face of controversy. Her signature low cut and tinted hairstyle give her a carefree look, and her infectious smile has endeared her to many fans.

However, her beauty has not shielded her from negative comments from the public. Nancy has faced her fair share of negativity, like when rumours circulated online that she had undergone butt surgery. Some fans were quick to criticise, but let's remember that it's never okay to judge someone based on their appearance.

Nancy has acted in several movies and TV shows, including "Hire a Man," "On the Real," "Living in Bondage: Breaking Free," “Blood Sisters” and the recent series "Shanty Town."

In the latter, Nancy got backlash on Twitter after a video of a nude woman purported to be her was circulated online. She had to come out and clarify that the person in the video was not her, but a body double.

Nancy's ability to withstand negative comments and move past controversies shows uncommon strength, and that is what we find truly beautiful.

7) Bimbo Ademoye

Bimbo Ademoye is the epitome of beauty, an embodiment of curves with an impossible figure-eight shape. Fans are always in awe of her tiny waist and curvy hips, often commenting under her social media posts with "Bimbo, where is your waist?"

She's been very open about how she maintains her figure, which she says is all-natural and achieved through regular workouts and waist trainers.

But Bimbo's talent as an actress is just as impressive as her beauty. She has a knack for bringing characters to life on screen and has delivered some unforgettable performances.

One of her most memorable roles was in the hit movie "Anikulapo," where she played a complex character with so much depth and nuance. She also shone in "Gangs of Lagos," proving her versatility as an actress.

But beyond her acting talent and stunning physique, Bimbo has an infectious personality. Her comedic nature, smile, bubbly personality, and positive energy make her a joy to be around.
